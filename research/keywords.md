# Character Framework

## Book

### Core Themes

* **Pressure** — pulled from every direction.
* **Loneliness** — no one to truly lean on.
* **Masks / Constraints** — unable to be authentic, unable to say everything.

---

# Kate (CEO)

### Keywords

* **Approval** — lives for others' validation.
* **Control / Perfectionism / Distrust / Responsibility** — must keep everything under control.
* **Emptiness** — success no longer feels fulfilling.

### Inner Arc

* **Lie:** If I don't control everything, everything will fall apart.
* **Fear:** They'll discover I'm not enough.
* **Desire:** To have one person with whom she can finally take off the mask.
* **Defense Mechanism:** Control every detail, every decision, every outcome.
* **Breaking Point:** She loses control despite doing everything right.

---

# Alex (CTO)

### Keywords

* **Impostor Syndrome / Fear / Anxiety** — afraid of not being enough.
* **Vulnerability / Misunderstood / Self-Doubt** — unable to express who he is.
* **Isolation** — lives only for work.

### Inner Arc

* **Lie:** One mistake will prove I never belonged here.
* **Fear:** Being exposed as an impostor.
* **Desire:** To be respected for who he is.
* **Defense Mechanism:** Retreats into technology, where everything is logical.
* **Breaking Point:** Realizes the problem is not technical—it is human.

---

# Michael (COO)

### Keywords

* **Decline / Obsolescence / Resignation / Burnout** — feels his time is running out.
* **Regret / Time / Stagnation** — realizes what he has lost.
* **Mask** — hides pain behind humor.

### Inner Arc

* **Lie:** My best years are behind me.
* **Fear:** Becoming irrelevant.
* **Desire:** To matter beyond his title.
* **Defense Mechanism:** Turns everything into a joke.
* **Breaking Point:** Realizes he has spent his entire life postponing happiness until "later."

---

# Nik (CFO)

### Keywords

* **Exhaustion** — carries more than he can bear.
* **Outsider / Shame / Self-Doubt / Fragility** — afraid he doesn't truly belong.
* **Pretending / Exaggeration** — projects a bigger version of himself than he really is.

### Inner Arc

* **Lie:** If people see the real me, they'll reject me.
* **Fear:** That he will always remain an outsider.
* **Desire:** To truly belong.
* **Defense Mechanism:** Exaggerates his achievements, compares himself to others, and constantly proves his worth.
* **Breaking Point:** His carefully constructed image collapses, leaving him with no choice but to show who he really is.
